About Nooh Floor Mill

Your trusted partner in quality wheat and flour products

Leading Flour Mill in Sindh

Nooh Floor Mill Private Limited is a premier wheat and flour supplier located in Tando Allahyar, Sindh. Under the leadership of our CEO, Mehtab Daudpota, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the flour milling industry.

We specialize in processing high-quality wheat into premium flour, offering various packaging options to meet diverse customer needs. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for businesses and households alike.
